## Overview

The dashboard is your home page in Swashbuckler. It shows two sections: **Pinned** entries for quick access to your most important content, and **Recent** entries so you can pick up where you left off.

{/* screenshot: Dashboard showing Pinned and Recent sections with entry cards */}

## Pinned Section

The Pinned section shows entries you've explicitly pinned for quick access. Pins are **per-user** — in a shared space, each collaborator has their own set of pinned entries.

### Pinning an Entry

1. Open an entry in the editor
2. Click the **pin icon** in the entry header

The entry immediately appears in the Pinned section on your dashboard. You can also pin entries from the sidebar by hovering over an entry and clicking the pin icon.

### Unpinning an Entry

Click the pin icon again (it appears filled when pinned) to remove the entry from the Pinned section.

### Empty State

If you haven't pinned anything yet, the Pinned section shows a message with a hint: "No pinned entries yet. Pin entries for quick access."

{/* screenshot: Empty pinned section with hint text */}

## Recent Section

The Recent section shows your most recently edited entries, ordered by last update time. It updates automatically as you work.

### Empty State

If you're in a new space with no entries yet, the Recent section shows: "No entries yet."

{/* screenshot: Empty recent section with hint text */}

## Navigation

Click any entry in either section to open it in the editor. The dashboard is always accessible from the **Home** link in the sidebar.
